50 DESCRIPTION
51  These functions compute the complex natural (base-<[e]>) logarithm
52  of <[z]>, with a branch cut along the negative real axis.
53 
54  <<clogf>> is identical to <<clog>>, except that it performs
55  its calculations on <<floats complex>>.
56 
57 RETURNS
58  @ifnottex
59  The clog functions return the complex natural logarithm value, in
60  the range of a strip mathematically unbounded along the real axis
61  and in the interval [-i*pi , +i*pi] along the imaginary axis.
62  @end ifnottex
63  @tex
64  The clog functions return the complex natural logarithm value, in
65  the range of a strip mathematically unbounded along the real axis
66  and in the interval [$-i\pi$, $+i\pi$] along the imaginary axis.
67  @end tex
68 
69 PORTABILITY
70  <<clog>> and <<clogf>> are ISO C99
71 
72 QUICKREF

83  double complex w;

86  rr = cabs(z);
