18  * Complex circular arc tangent
19  *
20  *
21  * SYNOPSIS:
22  *
23  * double complex catan();
24  * double complex z, w;
25  *
26  * w = catan (z);
27  *
28  *
29  * DESCRIPTION:
30  *
31  * If
32  * z = x + iy,
33  *
34  * then
35  * 1 ( 2x )
36  * Re w = - arctan(-----------) + k PI
37  * 2 ( 2 2)
38  * (1 - x - y )
39  *
40  * ( 2 2)
41  * 1 (x + (y+1) )
42  * Im w = - log(------------)
43  * 4 ( 2 2)
44  * (x + (y-1) )
45  *
46  * Where k is an arbitrary integer.
47  *
48  * catan(z) = -i catanh(iz).
49  *
50  * ACCURACY:
51  *
52  * Relative error:
53  * arithmetic domain # trials peak rms
